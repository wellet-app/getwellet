Founder

Download headshot

Betsy Eble

Founder & Clinical Designer

Betsy designed the UX for Duke Health’s first EHR, launched through Duke Executive Health. When she became a family caregiver, she saw the gap firsthand — every tool was built for the patient or the provider, but nothing was built for the person in between.

Wellet is the tool she needed and couldn’t find. It reads a loved one’s medical records, follows their medications and health patterns, and tells families what’s changing in plain language.

Boilerplate

Wellet is a health companion built for family caregivers. It reads uploaded records and connects to supported patient portals, follows medications and health patterns, and tells family members what’s changing in plain language. Wellet is built to help coordinate care — not to provide medical advice or second-guess clinicians. Founded by Betsy Eble, who designed the UX for Duke Health’s first EHR. Learn more at getwellet.com.
