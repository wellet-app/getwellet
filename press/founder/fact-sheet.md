## What Wellet does

Wellet reads a loved one's medical records, follows their medications and health patterns, and tells family members what's changing — in plain language. It's built to help coordinate care, not to second-guess providers.

**Core features**

- Upload documents (discharge summaries, labs, notes) for AI-powered extraction
- Connect directly to supported patient portals via standards-based EHR integration
- Ask Wellet — a caregiver-focused AI assistant that answers questions using only your loved one's records
- CareSignals — noticing shifts in medications, labs, and vitals over time
- Records — a plain-language view of visits, medications, and health history

## Who it's for

Family caregivers at every stage — adult children coordinating a parent's care, spouses managing a partner's chronic condition, siblings sharing responsibility across distance, and people staying on top of their own health alongside a loved one's.

## Founder

**Betsy Eble** designed the UX for Duke Health's first EHR, launched through Duke Executive Health. She founded Wellet after becoming a family caregiver and finding no tool built for the person in between the patient and the provider.

## Privacy & security

- Data is encrypted and isolated with row-level security
- EHR connections use standards-based OAuth via patient portals
- Wellet never shares individual records with advertisers or third parties

## Key milestones
